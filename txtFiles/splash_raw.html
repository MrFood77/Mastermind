Welcome to mastermind

CodeMaker
1. Make a code.
2. Wait for your opponent to guess your code.
3. Your opponent has 8 guesses.
4. If they can't break the code, you win.

CodeBreaker
1. Wait for your opponent to make a code.
2. Guess the code made by the opponent.
3. You have 8 guesses.
4. If you break the code, you win.
